Our Safer Return to Campus

Safely Together

This fall, we continue to prioritize the health and safety of our campus while achieving our No. 1 goal of Student Success. To that end, we are offering a range of class options: in-person, hybrid, fully remote. All offices will be open during regular business hours with most continuing to offer virtual/remote services as well.

Health Screener

All students, faculty, staff and auxiliary staff must complete the online Health Screener each time prior to coming to campus.

Face coverings are required inside buildings and facilities on campus.

Guests and visitors to the campus must follow the protocols and complete a separate health screener.
