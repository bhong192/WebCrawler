On August 18, 2021, President Coley ushered in the new academic year at a virtual edition of Fall Convocation. Faculty and Staff joined online to hear the State of the University address, with a special emphasis on our progress over the past year and how the perseverance of our faculty, staff, and students is keeping Cal Poly Pomona strong during challenging times.
