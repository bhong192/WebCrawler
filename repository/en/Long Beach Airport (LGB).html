Airport Updates Jun 18 2021
Long Beach Airport Offers New Hawaii Travel Resources in Time for Summer
Long Beach Airport (LGB) released a video today that highlights the Airport’s new position as a major launching point for travel to Hawaii. The video is available on the recently developed  webpage, which serves as a repository for travel tips out of LGB, flight schedules and suggested itineraries for travelers planning a vacation to the islands.
Airport Updates Apr 30 2021
Long Beach Airport Sets Emissions Reduction Target, Begins Carbon Accreditation Process

 Long Beach Airport (LGB) will enter the  (ACA) program and pledges to reduce its greenhouse gas emissions on a per passenger basis by 20% in 2025 and 40% in 2030. Administered by Airports Council International (ACI), the ACA program helps airports identify, manage and reduce their carbon emissions. 

Airport Updates Mar 31 2021
Long Beach Airport Seeks Development Proposals for 27 Acres

The City of Long Beach released a Request for Proposals (RFP) today for aeronautical development of all or portions of approximately 27 acres at Long Beach Airport (LGB). The availability of such a large amount of land is rare, with the last large-scale development at the Airport taking place in the 1980s and early 1990s.
