(Student Engagement) CHASS Series

Do you wonder how you can earn academic credit in the internships you work? Are you a CHASS major? Attend this UCDC/UCCS info session to learn about academic internship programs located in our state's and nation's capitals and how they relate to CHASS students!

The UCDC Program offers qualified undergraduate students from any major an exciting opportunity to combine course work, field research, and professional experience during a quarter's residence in our nation's capital.

Would you like to be an active part of your community? Would you be interested in designing and teaching your very own class? Find out how by attending this session to learn more about opportunities to engage in community service and how easy it is to create and teach your own R'course. 

Do you want to learn more about how you can engage in undergraduate research? You can apply for mini-grants, present at the undergraduate research symposium, publish in an academic journal for undergraduate students, AND earn national/prestigious awards! Find out how by attending this session.
