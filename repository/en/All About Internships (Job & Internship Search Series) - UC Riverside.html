All About Internships (Job & Internship Search Series)

An internship is a great way to “test out” a potential career path to see if it’s right for you. As an intern you will gain experience that will help you land your dream job after graduation while possibly earning academic credit. We will discuss: how to find an internship, getting academic credit for your internship, and find out why internships are so important to your success. Interested in attending?
