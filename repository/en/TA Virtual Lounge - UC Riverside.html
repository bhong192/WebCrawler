TA Virtual Lounge

XCITE offers weekly office hours for Graduate TAs who need guidance and support for special and unique issues that evolve throughout Fall 2020. Due to the continuing fluidity of instruction due to Covid19, TAs are most likely to be asked to alter their past duties and may feel overwhelmed or need assistance that is different from previous quarters. Senior Consultants Dr. Vanessa Ament and Cheryl Diermyer will be available for TAs to help navigate these unusual times and offer suggestions that are specifically targeting for these TAs.
