Wednesday Wellness Walks

by

Wed, Oct 6, 2021

11:30 AM – 1 PM PDT (GMT-7)

Meet outside Zot n' Go

G319 Student Center, Irvine, CA 92697, United States

Details

Please join us and enjoy the beauty of our campus and our local environment! All are welcome, including undergrads, grad students, faculty, and staff! Meet CSWHP and Campus Recreation staff outside of Zot N Go at 11:30AM and we will begin our stroll which will incorporate mindfulness, meditation and ecological education. Enjoy the company of and meet new students, staff and faculty. Go at your own pace and feel free to leave if needed.

Fall 2021 Schedule
*All walks meet in front of Zot N’ Go at 11:30AM

October 6: UCI Aldrich park
October 20: Mason Park
November 3: UCI Arobretum
November 17: Sea & Sage Wildlife Sanctuary
December 1: UCI Ecological Preserve

If there are any questions, please contact Doug Everhart, everhart@uci.edu.
