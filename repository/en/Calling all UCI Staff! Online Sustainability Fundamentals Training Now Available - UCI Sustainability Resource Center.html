Calling all UCI Staff! Online Sustainability Fundamentals Training Now Available

 

Would you like to learn how to incorporate more sustainable practices into your work?

Complete our online designed for all UCI staff members. Whether you work on campus, remotely, or hybrid, this training provides an overview of simple sustainable actions and helpful resources applicable to all work types.

This training includes the Sustainability Fundamentals presentation followed by a certification quiz.
