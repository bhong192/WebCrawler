Safer Return to Campus
Safer Return at Cal Poly Pomona

Phases of Safer Return

The university has developed a phased roadmap for our return to campus that is aligned with public health guidelines. Each phase includes our approach to teaching, learning, working, campus life and campus operations.

March 13 - August 16, 2020

Phase 1

Spring and Summer 2020

March 13 - August 16, 2020

Classes: About 98% virtual

Work and Offices: Only essential employees working on campus

Events and Activities: All virtual

Travel: Only essential with prior approval

Campus Access/Health Screener: Pre-authorization and health screener required

Guest and Visitor Access: Only essential with prior approval. Health screener required

What to Expect

August 17, 2020 - August 1, 2021

Phase 2

Fall 2020, Spring 2021 & Summer 2021 

August 17, 2020 - August 1, 2021

Classes: About 93% virtual

Work and Offices: Limited in-person services

Events and Activities: Very limited in-person events, with safety measures

Travel: Only essential with prior approval

Campus Access/Health Screener: Pre-authorization and health screener required

Guest and Visitor Access: Only essential with prior approval. Health screener required

What to Expect

  • Library Pilot Program 2
  • Service Learning & Internships (Off Campus)
  • Rose Float Lab

August 2, 2021 - Future

Phase 3

Fall 2021

August 2, 2021 - Future

Classes: At least 51% of classes with in-person component

Work and Offices: All offices and services open. Faculty and staff working on campus.

Events and Activities: Outdoor and small gatherings, with safety measures that may include RSVPs

Travel: Only essential with prior approval

Campus Access/Health Screener: No pre-authorization. Health screener required

Guest and Visitor Access: Permitted. Health screener required

What to Expect

  • Fall Classes Begin
  • BSC Study Space Pilot Reopening
