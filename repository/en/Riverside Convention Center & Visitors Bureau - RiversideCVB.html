Maps, Directions & Transportation

The hub of inland Southern California

Riverside is located just 20 miles from Ontario International Airport (ONT), home to seven major airlines, serving fourteen destinations with non-stop service, and offering 400 weekly departures. ONT makes travel easy, offering large airport amenities with small airport convenience, including easy exit and entrance, quick ticketing and gate access.

With its central location, Riverside is also served by Los Angeles International Airport – LAX (50 miles), John Wayne Airport, Orange County – SNA (40 miles), Long Beach Airport – LGB (45 miles) and Palm Spring International Airport – PSP (50 miles).

Riverside is also served by Uber, Lyft, Metrolink, Amtrak, Super Shuttle, limousines, and taxi services.

 

The 25 Suburbs Where Millennials Are Moving

Time Magazine Hails Riverside

Thousands of millennials are moving to the suburbs of Riverside, Calif., San Antonio, Texas and Orlando, Fla. The burbs of those three metro areas saw the greatest growth in the number of adults aged 25 to 34 between 2010 and 2015, according to data from the Urban Land Institute provided to TIME.
