Orange County is our Classroom
Our partnerships with school districts and community organizations address the needs and goals of local schools, while simultaneously inspiring our students and imbuing them with the skills necessary to transform education.
Advancing Educational Science
With more than $96 million in active grant funding, our faculty and students engage in research that advances educational science, learning outcomes and equity for all students.
Transforming Education
Doctoral students work closely with world-renowned faculty and learn the myriad skills necessary to become equity-focused educators dedicated to improving educational opportunities and outcomes.
Inclusive of All
Students in our Master’s of Arts in Teaching program earn a master’s degree and teaching credential in 14 months. Graduates are equipped to become change agents in diverse communities statewide.
The Future of Education
Students in the Education Sciences – the first of its kind in the nation – engage in an interdisciplinary exploration of the field of education in schools, communities, and society
