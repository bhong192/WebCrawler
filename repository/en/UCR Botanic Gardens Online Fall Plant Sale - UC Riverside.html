UCR Botanic Gardens Online Fall Plant Sale

The fall sale will feature Gardens-propagated horticultural and native plants, cacti and succulents, patio and house plants, water efficient plants, colorful landscape trees, and numerous pollinator-attracting species. The plant list will be on the website prior to the sale.

Friends members will enjoy early access at the online sale on 10/9/2021. Faculty and staff receive 10% discount on membership with code FS10. Join or renew by 10/2/2021.

10/9/2021, 6am-midnight- Friends of the UCRBG members' sale.

10/10/2021, 6am-midnight- Public sale.
