2021-2022 Florence Bayz Music Series — Wednesday@Noon via Zoom Series

Due to COVID 19, the Florence Bayz Music -Wednesday@Noon via Zoom Series offers online concerts, lectures, and presentations of academic research by Department of Music faculty, postdoctoral researchers, students, and international guest artists and scholars. Dana Kaufman, coordinator. 

Events are free and open to the public.

A link to the Zoom meeting will be provided.
