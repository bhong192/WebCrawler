UCR Hospitality Services for Fall 2021 and beyond

As with each department on campus, the circumstances surrounding the pandemic have had profound impacts on the UCR Hospitality Services business. As campus events have not been held since March 2020, our team has been working in different capacities around campus and will need time to rebuild. As such, it will take some time to make the necessary adjustments to achieve our pre-pandemic service standards of excellence. Operational capacity may be reduced and adjusted accordingly.

Knowing this, we welcome and appreciate your business going forward. Please inquire early and we thank you in advance for your patience as we work ourselves back to full capacity in meeting your event needs.

Hospitality Services

UC Riverside Hospitality Services provides the campus and its surrounding communities with a variety of services and venues for social, cultural, educational, and corporate events. This includes events such as weddings, anniversaries, and birthday parties.

Beautiful, Affordable Wedding Venues
The  and the Barn, our two wedding venues at UC Riverside, are ideal for intimate- and medium-sized weddings and receptions. Both venues are competitively priced with packages starting at $500.

Venues, Catering and Everything You Need
Whether it's a business dinner, a charity function, a holiday party, or another type of special event – our event coordinators can create a package perfect for your special event at one of our campus venues. Services may include catering, audio/visual, guest parking, floral arrangements and specialty linen. 

Plan a Summer Conference at UC Riverside
Whether it’s a small or large group, UC Riverside offers competitively priced packages and comfortable accommodations for summer conferences and retreats. Summer conference packages include your choice of overnight accommodations, all-you-care-to-eat meals, and guest parking. For events, find plenty of additional meeting and recreational space for your entire group.

Meetings and More!
Our venues feature a variety of meetings spaces that can be reserved for multiple- or single-part workshops, seminars, training sessions, presentations and other types of meetings. A wide-range of setups are available in each meeting space, creating flexible and comfortable spaces for participants.

Student Organization Events
Affordable meeting spaces are available to host your student events on campus. Based on availability, meeting spaces may be booked for single or recurring events. All meeting spaces include tables, chairs and most include audio/visual. We offer student discounts on meeting space reservations in certain locations.
