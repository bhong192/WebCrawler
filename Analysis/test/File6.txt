 Bronco ScholarWorks 
                                                        
        
               
          
       
        
        
    
    
                                                                                Bronco ScholarWorks is Cal Poly Pomona’s institutional repository, which includes student research projects, student theses, and faculty created learning materials.  
                                                        
                                                
                                                                              
                        
                        
                                                
                         
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Affordable Learning Initiative 
                                                        
        
               
          
       
        
        
    
    
                                                                                Faculty and students have been voicing their concern about the high cost of textbooks. The ALI project is our response to this concern.  
                                                        
                                                
                                                                              
                        
                        
                                                
                         
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Special Collections
