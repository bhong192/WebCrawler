 Campus Budget Update 
 
 
 
 
 At the beginning of the new academic year, President Soraya Coley and other members of university leadership will share a budget overview with the campus, including an update on the State and CSU budgets and the resulting impact for the campus. This year’s conversation will be held in a virtual format. Joining her will be Interim Provost Iris Levine, Vice President for Administration & Finance and CFO Ysabel Trinidad, Associate Vice President for Finance & Administrative Services Joe Simonseschi and Vice President for Student Affairs Christina Gonzales. 
 When:  Oct 26, 2021 12:00 PM Pacific Time (US and Canada)  Topic:  Campus Conversation on the Budget Passcode:  CPP 
 A video of the campus conversation, as well as a PDF of the presentation, will be available for download post-event (authentication required).
